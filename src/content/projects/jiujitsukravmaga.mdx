A landing page for a martial arts school offering Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Krav Maga. The brief was simple: prospective students should be able to learn about the school, check the weekly schedule and reach out — no sign-ups, no booking flows, just the essentials.

## Sections

- **Home** — school introduction and highlights.
- **Classes and schedule** — class descriptions paired with an **interactive slideshow** and the weekly timetable.
- **Contact** — contact details with a **Google Maps** embed and social media links.

## Stack

Built with **Astro** for static-first rendering, **Tailwind** for styling and a bit of **JavaScript** for the interactive pieces. The result is a site that loads fast on mobile — which is where most visitors arrive.
